More about me

During my elementary to college years, she has been actively engaging herself in different clubs and organizations with various platforms that address SDGs and student welfare. She is also a multi-awardee student and student leadership awardee. She has competed in research for National and International. She is serving the country since she was in Grade School — in her 5th Grade, to be exact.

Currently, she is the Charter President of Kiwanis Club of Balintawak founded last 2019. She is also an Internal Director of JCI QC Diamante.A Director of the Earth Shaker Philippines. A Membership Director of Rotaract Club of San Francisco Del Monte Malaya Achievers. A Research Committee of Youth Advocate for the Philippines, also known as YAP

By profession, she is a Business Person in Globe Auto Supply and Apex Mill Supply, and she is a Full-Stack Web Developer and founded Cheng Kong Technologies — an organization that helps Sustainable Development Goals using Web Development.

She is not just an advocate for research and innovation but she is also an advocate for the Filipinos to become more and believe in themselves that they can contribute to the SDG by helping society.
